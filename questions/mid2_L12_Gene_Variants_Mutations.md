**Q1.** Which of the following best describes a nonsense mutation?

A) p.Arg117His
B) A substitution creating a premature stop codon
C) UGA, UAA, and UAG
D) FBN1

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** من L12: "Nonsense (Stopgain) Variant: A substitution that leads to the generation of one of the stop codons (UAA, UAG, or UGA) → Will result in a premature termination of translation (truncated protein)."

**Q2.** Which of the following variants is an example of a missense mutation?

C) p.Arg117His
D) Familial hypercholesterolemia

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** من L12: Missense = استبدال حمض أميني بآخر. p.Arg117His = Arginine استبدلت بـ Histidine في موضع 117. - p.Gly542* = Nonsense (علامة * تعني stop codon) - c.489+1G>T = Splice site mutation - p.Phe508del = Frameshift/deletion (CF الطفرة الشائعة)

**Q4.** Which of the following disorders is most commonly caused by loss of function of the protein product?

A) Insertion of 3 amino acids
B) UGA, UAA, and UAG
C) When a child inherits an autosomal recessive disorder from both parents, who have different mutations on the same gene
D) Familial hypercholesterolemia

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:** من L12: "Loss-of-function variants → haploinsufficiency → familial hypercholesterolemia" (نقص مستقبل LDL). أما Huntington وAchondroplasia = gain-of-function، Marfan = dominant-negative.

**Q5.** When a mutant gene product interferes with the function of the normal protein in a heterozygous state, this is known as:

A) Transition
B) Degree of relationship to the index case
C) Dominant-negative effect
D) A substitution creating a premature stop codon

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** من L12: "Dominant-Negative Variants: When a mutant gene in the heterozygous state interferes with the function of the normal gene product → results in loss of protein activity or function. E.g., osteogenesis imperfecta and Marfan syndrome."

**Q7.** Which sequence correctly describes the major DNA repair mechanism?

C) Familial hypercholesterolemia
D) HbH disease
