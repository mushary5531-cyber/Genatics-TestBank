**Q1.** What are the Potential Reasons for Pediatric Genetic Counseling?

A) Preimplantation genetic diagnosis
B) Folic acid
C) Cystic fibrosis
D) Child with a family history of a genetic condition

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:** Pediatric genetic counseling يُشمل: أطفال بتشوهات، تأخر نمو، أطفال ذوي تاريخ عائلي لحالات جينية. Adult onset يُعالَج بـ adult genetic counseling.

**Q2.** According to genetic counseling, all the following is true EXCEPT:

A) Genetic counseling is directive
B) Hypogonadism
C) Sanger sequencing
D) Folic acid

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** من L14: Genetic counseling هو Non-directive (لا يُعطي توجيهات مباشرة). يُقدم المعلومات ليتخذ الفرد قراره بنفسه.

**Q3.** Two parents came to counseling. They have 2 affected children with Hemophilia A. They want a healthy child. What to recommend?

A) Preimplantation genetic diagnosis
B) Genetic counseling is directive
C) Cystic fibrosis
D) Sanger Sequencing

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** PGD = فحص الجنين قبل الزرع في أثناء IVF لاختيار الأجنة غير المصابة.

**Q4.** A 30-year-old male with chronic pulmonary disease and infertility. Possible diagnosis?

A) Prader-Willi
B) Cystic fibrosis
C) Folic acid
D) Child with a family history of a genetic condition

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** CF في الذكور يُسبب: مرض رئوي مزمن + عقم ذكوري بسبب CBAVD (congenital bilateral absence of vas deferens).

**Q5.** Medication given in first trimester of pregnancy?

A) Cystic fibrosis
B) Folic acid
C) Sanger Sequencing
D) Down syndrome pregnancies

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** حمض الفوليك يُعطى في الأشهر الأولى (periconceptional) لتقليل خطر NTD. Streptomycin وIsoniazid خطرة في الحمل.

**Q6.** A male with TB disease using isoniazid and having severe adverse effects:

A) Folic acid
B) Sanger Sequencing
C) Change the drug
D) Child with a family history of a genetic condition

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** Isoniazid metabolism يختلف حسب NAT2 phenotype (slow vs fast acetylators). في Slow acetylators قد تحدث سمية عصبية. السبب pharmacogenetics.

**Q7.** An increase in inhibin-A level in maternal serum is a diagnosis of?

A) Genetic counseling is directive
B) Down syndrome pregnancies
C) Maternal serum AFP
D) Cystic fibrosis

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** Maternal serum screening: في Down syndrome ترتفع: inhibin-A وhCG. تنخفض: AFP وEstriol.

**Q8.** Which of the following prenatal tests doesn't carry a risk of fetal loss?

A) Maternal serum AFP
B) Change the drug
C) Hypogonadism
D) Child with a family history of a genetic condition

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** Maternal serum AFP هو فحص دم بسيط للأم ولا يحمل أي خطر على الجنين. أما CVS وAmniocentesis وFetal tissue sampling فكلها تحمل خطر إجهاض.
