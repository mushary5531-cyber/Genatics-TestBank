**Q1.** What is the definition of translocation?

A) Aneuploidy
B) Genetic testing for the mother
C) Exchange of genetic material between non-homologous chromosomes
D) Male with reciprocal translocation between chromosome 7 and chromosome 11

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** الـ Translocation = انتقال قطعة من كروموسوم إلى كروموسوم آخر غير متماثل. Reciprocal translocation = تبادل متبادل، Robertsonian = التصاق كروموسومين acrocentric عند الـ centromere.

**Q2.** 46,XY, t(7;11) — What does this mean?

A) Male with reciprocal translocation between chromosome 7 and chromosome 11
B) 46,XX,rob(14;21)+21
C) G6PD deficiency
D) 5p deletion

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** t = translocation، (7;11) = بين كروموسوم 7 و11، 46,XY = ذكر بـ 46 كروموسوم → balanced reciprocal translocation.

**Q3.** What is a characteristic of balanced translocation?
