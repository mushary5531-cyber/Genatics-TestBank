**Q1.** Which of the following best describes the central dogma of molecular biology?

A) DNA → Protein → RNA
B) RNA → DNA → Protein
C) DNA → RNA → Protein
D) Protein → RNA → DNA

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** The central dogma states that genetic information flows from DNA to RNA (transcription) and then from RNA to Protein (translation).

**Q2.** A codon consists of how many nucleotide bases?

A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** A codon is a triplet of nucleotide bases that codes for a specific amino acid or stop signal.
