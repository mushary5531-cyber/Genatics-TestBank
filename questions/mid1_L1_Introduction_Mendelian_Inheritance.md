**Q1.** What is an alternative form of a gene called?

A) Pleiotropy
B) All daughters affected, none of the sons affected
C) Allele
D) Chromosome

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** الـ Allele هو شكل بديل لجين معين يوجد في نفس المكان (Locus) على الكروموسوم المتماثل. من L1: "Gene is a unit of hereditary information that occupies a fixed position (locus) on a chromosome."

**Q2.** What is the definition of a gene?

A) 50%
B) Paternal chromosome 15 deletion
C) A hereditary unit of DNA that encodes a functional product
D) 50% for each pregnancy

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** من L1: "Gene is a unit of heredity which is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring. Genes achieve their effects by directing the synthesis of proteins."

**Q3.** What is meant by a gene variant (mutation)?

A) CAG
B) Allele
C) A permanent change in the DNA sequence
D) Oocyte

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** من L1: "Gene Variant is a permanent alteration in the DNA sequence that makes up a gene, such that the sequence differs from what is found in most people."

**Q4.** What is the terminology that describes the total genetic heritage material of a cell or organism?

**Q5.** The unit that contains nucleic acid and protein and is responsible for heredity is called?

**Q6.** Which of the following diseases is an example of pleiotropy?
