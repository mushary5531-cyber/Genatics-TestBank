**Q1.** What method can be used to detect DMD?

A) MLPA
B) Sanger sequencing
C) Paternity exclusion
D) They contain a plasmid with genes for antibiotic resistance

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** من L13: "Example of disease which can be diagnosed with MLPA: Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD)." MLPA يكتشف الـ deletions والـ duplications على مستوى الـ exons.

**Q2.** Which is NOT required for a PCR reaction?

A) Paternity exclusion
B) Dideoxy-dNTPs
C) Autosomal recessive
D) Whole exome sequencing

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** من L13: مكونات PCR: Template DNA + Primers + dNTPs + Thermostable DNA polymerase (Taq) + Buffer. أما ddNTPs (dideoxy) فتُستخدم في Sanger Sequencing وليس PCR العادي.

**Q3.** The polymerase chain reaction for amplification of DNA sequences relies upon the use of:

A) DNA polymerase
B) Heat-stable DNA polymerase
C) Sanger sequencing
D) Selective amplification of a specific DNA sequence

**Answer:** B
**Explanation:** من L13: PCR يستخدم Taq polymerase (مستخلصة من Thermus aquaticus) لأنها تتحمل درجات الحرارة العالية في كل دورة من دورات PCR.

**Q4.** Which of the following methods is used for separation of DNA fragments?

A) They contain a plasmid with genes for antibiotic resistance
B) Selective amplification of a specific DNA sequence
C) Gel electrophoresis
D) Newborn screening of galactosemia

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** Gel electrophoresis يفصل شظايا DNA حسب الحجم. PCR = تضخيم، Restriction digestion = قطع، Bacterial transformation = إدخال DNA للبكتيريا.

**Q5.** What is the correct sequence of events in Southern blotting?

A) Heat-stable DNA polymerase
B) Newborn screening of galactosemia
C) Separation of DNA fragments by electrophoresis → transfer to a membrane → hybridization with a labelled probe sequence
D) MLPA

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** Southern blot خطوات: 1. قطع DNA بـ restriction enzymes 2. فصل الشظايا بـ gel electrophoresis 3. نقل (transfer) للـ membrane (نيتروسيليلوز) 4. Hybridization مع probe مُعلَّم (labeled) 5. كشف الإشارة

**Q6.** What is the natural function of restriction enzymes?

A) Newborn screening of galactosemia
B) They contain a plasmid with genes for antibiotic resistance
C) Selective amplification of a specific DNA sequence
D) Protecting bacteria by cleaving the DNA of infecting viruses

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:** Restriction enzymes في البكتيريا تقطع DNA الغريب (الفيروسات) لحماية البكتيريا. أما DNA البكتيريا نفسها فيكون محمياً بالـ methylation.

**Q8.** What is the best genetic testing for chromosomal duplication < 3 mega base pair?

A) Microarray
B) Both parents are carriers and child C3 is normal
C) Separation of DNA fragments by electrophoresis → transfer to a membrane → hybridization with a labelled probe sequence
D) They contain a plasmid with genes for antibiotic resistance

**Answer:** A
**Explanation:** من L13: "CMA (Chromosomal Microarray) detects microdeletions/microduplications < 5Mb with resolution ~1Kb." الـ Conventional karyotyping لا يكتشف < 5-10Mb.

**Q9.** An 18-month-old with muscle weakness and frequent falls. Doctor suspects a point mutation on the SMN1 gene. How to confirm?

A) Autosomal recessive
B) Heat-stable DNA polymerase
C) Sanger Sequencing
D) Sanger sequencing

**Answer:** C
**Explanation:** من L13: Sanger sequencing يُستخدم للكشف عن point mutations في جين محدد. MLPA يكتشف deletions/duplications لا point mutations. (ملاحظة: SMN1 deletions تُكتشف بـ MLPA، لكن point mutations تحتاج Sanger)

**Q10.** A 2-year-old child has developmental delay and multiple congenital anomalies. Chromosome analysis and microarray are negative, no specific syndrome suspected. Most appropriate next test?

A) Klinefelter syndrome
B) Gel electrophoresis
C) Suspect 2
D) Whole exome sequencing

**Answer:** D
**Explanation:** من L13: WES يُستخدم عندما تفشل الفحوصات الأخرى. يكشف عن SNVs وsmall indels في جميع exons. يُستخدم بشكل خاص في DD/ID غير محدد السبب.

**Q11.** A woman with breast cancer is found to carry a pathogenic CHEK2 variant. Which test is most appropriate to screen her siblings?
